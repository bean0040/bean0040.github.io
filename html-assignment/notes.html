My notes about HTML tags

List of 5 HTML tags

Paragraph <p>
The browser will add a space before and after the content of the paragraph.
Emphasis <em>
A string of emphasized, slightly more important text. Screen readers will change their voice for this text.
Important text <strong>
A string of highly emphasized, much more important text. Screen readers will change their voice for this text.
Horizontal Line <hr>
Represents a thematic break in the content. For example, a scene change or topic change.
